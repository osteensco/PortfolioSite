An exciting and dynamic space shooter made with Python

Object Oriented Programming

This project displays a solid understanding of OOP with many objects interacting and altering each other. Most classes are child classes that utilize inheritance. There are menu classes to help provide navigation and creating a game instance, ship classes to handle boss's and enemies, drops, lasers, visual effects and more.

Dynamic Bosses

Each Boss you face is procedurally generated with a different body, weapon, and apparatus. Each boss weapon and apparatus have their own mechanics and nuances. For example, one of weapons may do extensively more damage and even cut through the toughest of defenses, while another will lie in wait while blending in with the vastness of space. The boss apparetuses can provide extra defense, spawn drones to help fight, or help turn the player's own attacks against them.

Power Up Drops

As enemies are defeated they have a change to drop capsules that will provide some sort of power boost to the player. The effects of these can be many different things from increasing the player's fire rate to providing a temporarily powerful shield that destroy's enemy ships and protects the player from (almost) anything.
