An exciting and dynamic space shooter made with Python

Object Oriented Programming

The game is made using OOP with parent and child classes utilizing inheritance. As an example, there are menu subclasses to help provide navigation, change settings, and creating a game instance. There is also a ship class with several sublasses to handle boss's and enemies, as well as the player's actions, score and health.

Dynamic Bosses

Each Boss you face is procedurally generated with a different body, weapon, and apparatus. Each boss weapon and apparatus have their own mechanics and nuances. For example, one of the weapons may do extensively more damage and even cut through the toughest of defenses, while another will lie in wait while blending in with the vastness of space. The boss apparetuses can provide extra defense, spawn drones to help fight, or help turn the player's own attacks against them.

Power Up Drops

As enemies are defeated they have a chance to drop capsules that will provide some sort of power boost to the player. The effects of these can be many different things from increasing the player's fire rate to providing a temporarily powerful shield that destroy's enemy ships and protects the player from (almost) anything.
